Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, how we protect it, and your choices, consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). The short version: we collect the minimum needed to run listings, reports, advertising, and billing; we never sell your data; and you can delete everything yourself from Settings.

2. What we collect

  • Account data: your Discord user ID, username, global display name, and avatar, received when you sign in with Discord OAuth. We do not receive your email address or password.
  • ER:LC API key: stored encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. It is decrypted only at the moment a report or connection test runs, and you can remove it at any time in Settings.
  • Event and report data: your listings and the statistics compiled into reports (joins, sessions, staff activity, mod calls, queue) as exposed by the PRC API for your server.
  • Delivery settings: a Discord webhook URL if you save one, your bot-DM opt-in choice, and any extra report-recipient Discord ID you attach to an event.
  • Advertising data: your ad creative, destination link, booking dates, and aggregate performance metrics (impressions, clicks, click-through). Click and impression logging is used to give advertisers their analytics and to detect abuse.
  • Payment data: handled entirely by Stripe (card) or Roblox (Robux). We store only your plan, its verification timestamp, the Stripe customer reference, and, for Robux verification, the Roblox user ID you supply. We never receive or store card numbers.
  • Technical and security data: coarse rate-limit counters keyed to IP addresses to prevent spam and botting, and an audit log of administrative and staff actions.

3. How we use it

To operate listings and the discovery feed; compile, deliver, and display your reports; run and measure advertising; process subscriptions and credit purchases; prevent abuse, spam, and fraud; and provide support. If AI summaries are enabled, your report's statistics (never your API key, and no card data) are sent to Anthropic's API solely to generate the summary text. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models.

4. Who we share it with

  • Service providers: Netlify (hosting and encrypted storage), Discord (login and report delivery), Stripe (card payments), Roblox (gamepass verification), PRC (your server's API), and Anthropic (AI summaries). Each receives only what its function requires. Some of these providers store or process data outside Australia (primarily the United States); by using the Service you acknowledge this overseas disclosure under APP 8.
  • Other users: your listings, host username, banner, approved advertisements, and any news you publish are public by design. Your reports are private to you and any extra recipient you choose.
  • We do not sell personal information. We disclose it to law-enforcement or authorities only where we are legally required to, or to protect the safety of users.

5. Security

Sessions use signed cookies marked HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite. ER:LC keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Staff and administrative actions require server-verified roles, are rate-limited, and are audit-logged; privileged access is scoped to the minimum each role needs. No system is perfectly secure, so if we become aware of an eligible data breach likely to result in serious harm, we will notify affected individuals and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

6. Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active. "Delete account" in Settings immediately and permanently removes your account record, your encrypted API key, and all of your events and reports. Aggregate advertising metrics, coarse rate-limit counters, and audit entries recording staff actions may be retained for security, billing, and fraud-prevention purposes for up to 12 months.
