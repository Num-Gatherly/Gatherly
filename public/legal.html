Privacy Policy

1. Overview

This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices, consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). The short version: we collect the minimum needed to run listings, reports, and billing, we never sell your data, and you can delete everything yourself from Settings.

2. What we collect

  • Account data: your Discord user ID, username, and avatar, received when you sign in with Discord OAuth. We do not receive your email address or password.
  • ER:LC API key: stored encrypted with AES-256-GCM. It is decrypted only at the moment a report or connection test runs, and you can remove it at any time in Settings.
  • Event and report data: your listings and the statistics compiled into reports (joins, sessions, staff activity, mod calls) as exposed by the PRC API for your server.
  • Delivery settings: a Discord webhook URL if you save one, your bot-DM opt-in choice, and any extra report recipient Discord ID you attach to an event.
  • Payment data: handled entirely by Stripe (card) or Roblox (Robux). We store only your plan, its verification timestamp, and, for Robux verification, the Roblox user ID you supply. We never receive card numbers.
  • Technical data: coarse rate-limit counters keyed to IP addresses to prevent abuse, and a staff audit log of administrative actions.

3. How we use it

To operate listings and the discovery feed, compile and deliver your reports, process subscriptions, prevent abuse, and provide support. If AI summaries are enabled, your report's statistics (not your API key) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate the summary text.

4. Who we share it with

  • Service providers: Netlify (hosting and storage), Discord (login and report delivery), Stripe (card payments), Roblox (gamepass verification), PRC (your server's API), and Anthropic (AI summaries). Each receives only what its function requires, and some are located outside Australia (primarily the United States).
  • Other users: your listings, host username, and banner are public by design. Your reports are private to you and any extra recipient you choose.
  • We do not sell personal information, and we disclose it to authorities only where the law requires.

5. Security

Sessions are signed cookies marked HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite. ER:LC keys are encrypted at rest. Admin actions require server-verified staff roles and are audit-logged. No system is perfectly secure, so if we become aware of a data breach likely to result in serious harm we will notify affected users and the OAIC as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

6. Retention and deletion

Data is kept while your account is active. "Delete account" in Settings immediately and permanently removes your account record, encrypted key, and all your events and reports. Coarse rate-limit counters and audit entries naming staff actions may persist for security purposes for up to 12 months.
