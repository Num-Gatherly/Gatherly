Security notice - read before you submit
Your ER:LC API key grants powerful access to your private server, including reading all server data and player activity. Never share this key outside of this domain. On Gatherly it is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM, stored in an isolated environment that no staff member or third party can read, and used only to pull statistics during your event window. Gatherly never sends commands to your server. This is governed by our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
